Refund Policy FOR धरोहर '24

At धरोहर '24, we are committed to providing a seamless experience for all attendees. Please carefully read our refund policy before booking your tickets.

1. Ticket Cancellation and Refund Eligibility:

- Refund Requests:

  • Refund requests will be accepted up to 7 days before the event date.
  • Refunds will not be processed for requests made after this period.
  • To request a refund, please contact us at dharoharhelp@gmail.com or via our Contact Us portal.

2. Event Postponement or Cancellation:

- If the event is cancelled:

  • If Dharohar 2024 is cancelled by the organizers for any reason, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • Refunds will be processed automatically within 7 days from the event cancellation notice.

- If the event is postponed:

  • If the event is postponed to another date, your ticket will remain valid for the new date.
  • If you are unable to attend the rescheduled event, you may request a full refund within 7 days of the new date announcement.

3. Transfer of Tickets:

  • Tickets are non-transferable under normal circumstances. However, if you are unable to attend Dharohar 2024, you may transfer your ticket to someone else within 7 days of the event by contacting us at dharoharhelp@gmail.com. We will process the transfer request at our discretion.

4. Refund Method:

  • Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase.
  • It may take up to 7 business days for the refund to reflect in your account after processing.

5. Special Cases:

- Health-related or Emergency Refunds:

  • If you are unable to attend due to a medical emergency or other serious reasons, please provide the necessary documentation (e.g., doctor’s note) and we may review your request for a partial or full refund.

- Invalid or Fraudulent Tickets:

  • Tickets purchased through unauthorized sources or found to be fraudulent will not be refunded or honored for entry to Dharohar 2024.

6. Non-Refundable Fees:

  • Please note that certain fees, such as processing or service fees, may not be eligible for a refund.

7. Terms of Use:

  • By purchasing a ticket, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this refund policy.
  • The event organizers reserve the right to update or modify this policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated to ticket holders via email and/or posted on our website.

Contact Us:

For further inquiries regarding cancellations and refunds, please reach out to our support team at dharoharhelp@gmail.com or Call Us at: +91 22 1234 5678 .
