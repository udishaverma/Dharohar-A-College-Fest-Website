IT'S GO TIME! WELCOME TO धरोहर 2024!

Dharohar is an exciting three-day festival at ABES Engineering College, celebrating culture, creativity, and innovation. Organized by students, it blends technical skills with artistic expression, attracting participants from across the country for a memorable experience.

The festival features thrilling attractions like a Ferris wheel and roller coaster, alongside interactive games and competitions. A celebrity guest adds extra excitement, while sponsors provide prizes and goodies, ensuring plenty of fun for all attendees.

At its core, Dharohar showcases vibrant cultural performances, including dance, music, and DJ acts. It's a platform for students to showcase their talents, connect with peers, and celebrate the spirit of student life.

The dance performance at Dharohar, a vibrant cultural fest, blends traditional and contemporary styles with energetic choreography, colorful costumes, and rhythmic music, captivating the audience with its artistry and passion.

The magic show at Dharohar enchants audiences with mind-bending illusions and mesmerizing tricks. Skilled magicians deliver awe-inspiring performances, blending mystery and entertainment seamlessly. Enhanced by stunning visuals and surprise elements, the show leaves spectators in awe, evoking wonder and amazement from start to finish.

The music show at Dharohar showcases soulful performances spanning genres from classical to contemporary. Talented musicians craft an enchanting atmosphere with melodic rhythms, celebrating diverse musical traditions and leaving the audience captivated by the magic of sound.

The handicraft workshops at Dharohar provide hands-on experiences in traditional crafts, including pottery, weaving, and painting. Guided by skilled artisans, participants celebrate creativity and craftsmanship, crafting unique pieces while immersing themselves in cultural heritage.
