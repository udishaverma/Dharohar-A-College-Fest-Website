Frequently Asked Questions

1. General Information

What is Dharohar?
Dharohar is our annual college fest celebrating cultural heritage, talent, and innovation. It features a variety of events, competitions, and performances by renowned artists.
When and where is Dharohar happening?
Dharohar will be held on 24 December, 2024 at ABES Engineering College,Ghaziabad. Visit our website for location details and directions.
Can non-students attend Dharohar?
Absolutely! Dharohar is open to students, alumni, and the general public.

2. Registration

How do I register for Dharohar?
You can register on our official website by filling out the registration form available under the "Get Tickets" tab.
Do I need to register separately for individual events?
Yes, each event has its own registration process. Check the event details on our website for more information.
Can I register on the day of the event?
While online registration is preferred, on-spot registration will be available for certain events, subject to availability.

3. Competitions and Events

What types of events are organized at Dharohar?
Dharohar features cultural performances, workshops, exciting games, food festivals, music concerts, and much more!
Can I participate in multiple events?
Yes, you can participate in multiple events, provided the schedules do not overlap.
How do I find the schedule for the fest?
The complete schedule is available on our website under the “Schedule” section.

4. Accommodations and Facilities

Is parking available at the venue?
Yes, parking facilities are available at the venue. Follow the signage for designated parking areas.
Will food be available at the venue?
Yes, a variety of food stalls will be set up at the fest, offering cuisines to suit all tastes.

5. Contact and Support

Who can I contact for more information?
You can reach out to us at dharoharhelp@gmail.com or call +912212345678 for any queries.
What should I do if I face issues with registration?
For registration issues, contact our support team at dharoharsupport@gmail.com.
